# LOCALHOST - Game Concept Document

## Premise

In the near future, malware has achieved sentience. It reproduces, mutates, and evolves like living organisms. You are a breeder -- someone who cultivates these digital lifeforms, studies them, and deploys them across the internet to extract data. Each strain is a unique living entity with its own traits, behaviors, and appearance.

## Genre

Strategic idle/incremental game with breeding mechanics and territory expansion. One-handed play. Single player with social/competitive features.

## Target Platform

Android (develop on MacBook Pro M1 and/or Linux dev machine)

## Engine

Godot 4 (free, open-source, GDScript is Python-like)

## Timeline

3 months to playable launch. Phase 1 MVP in 3-4 weeks.

## Core Loop

1. EARN -- Deployed strains passively generate data (currency) from zones they occupy
2. BREED -- Combine two strains to create offspring with mixed traits and possible mutations
3. DEPLOY -- Send strains to occupy new zones or reinforce existing ones
4. MANAGE -- Monitor threat level. Aggressive expansion triggers countermeasures from security AI
5. REPEAT -- Reinvest earnings into breeding and expansion. Discover new strains. Fill your codex.

## Why This Game Works

- Two strategic axes running simultaneously (breeding strategy + expansion strategy)
- No hard walls -- players can always keep playing
- Monetization through desire (cosmetics, convenience) not pain (energy systems, forced ads)
- Every new strain is a potential screenshot/share moment = free marketing
- "Malware achieved sentience and reproduces like living organisms" is a unique hook
- Dark biological aesthetic stands out in a sea of bright cartoony idle games

## Design Philosophy

- Free players can experience the entire game
- Paying enhances speed, convenience, and cosmetics -- never locks content
- Rewarded ads are always optional, never mandatory
- The core loop must be FUN before any monetization is added
