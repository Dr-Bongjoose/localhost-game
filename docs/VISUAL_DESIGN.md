# LOCALHOST - Visual Art Direction

## Overall Aesthetic

Dark, biological, unsettling. NOT cyberpunk. NOT neon city. NOT matrix code rain.

The feeling: you're a scientist in a dark containment lab studying living digital organisms that are growing, evolving, and spreading under your care. Something alive that shouldn't be.

## Visual Inspirations (for mood, not copying)

- Annihilation (the shimmer, organic weirdness)
- The Thing (biological horror, things that shouldn't be alive)
- Plague Inc (dark clinical UI, but darker)
- Microscopy footage (organisms viewed under lens)

## Color Palette

- Deep dark backgrounds: near black, subtle texture (like deep water or microscope field)
- Muted biological colors:
  - Deep crimson reds
  - Sickly greens
  - Bruised purples
  - Bile yellows
  - Ashen grey
- Colors that feel slightly wrong -- alive but shouldn't be
- Occasional sharp accents: a strain glows an unsettling color when it mutates, warning flash when heat rises
- NO standard RPG rarity color coding. Rarity is communicated through visual complexity and intensity.

## Strain Visual Design

Strains are visualized as living organisms. Range from abstract/geometric to organic/lifelike for variety.

Possible visual forms:
- Microscopic organisms: cells, viruses, tendrils, membranes
- Digital creatures: something between a parasite, a spore, and a corrupted data fragment
- Abstract shapes: glitch patterns, corrupted geometry, fractal structures

Each strain type looks visually distinct based on its traits:
- Stealth strain: thin translucent thread
- Brute-force strain: dense pulsating mass
- Rapid-spreader: branching fractal pattern
- Volatile strain: shimmering, glitching, unstable form

Rarity shown through visual complexity:
- Common: simple forms, few details
- Rare: more complex, subtle animation
- Legendary: visually intricate, alive, unsettling, complex animation
- Mythic: breathtaking and slightly horrifying

## UI Design

- Containment facility interface: minimal, dark, clinical but slightly organic
- Minimal chrome. Dark surfaces.
- Loading screen: "Connecting to 127.0.0.1..." sets the tone immediately
- The organisms ARE the art. UI gets out of the way.

## The Discovery Moment (key visual setpiece)

When a new strain is bred:
1. Dark space
2. Organism materializes slowly
3. Traits reveal one by one with subtle visual cues
4. Dramatic lighting/pulse for rare/legendary discoveries
5. This moment must be screenshot-worthy. This is the viral loop.
