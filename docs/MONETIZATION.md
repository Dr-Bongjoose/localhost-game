# LOCALHOST - Monetization Design

## Core Principle

Free players can experience everything. Paying enhances speed, convenience, and cosmetics. Never lock content behind a paywall. Lock CONVENIENCE behind a paywall.

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Rewarded Ads (always optional, never mandatory)

| Action | Reward |
|--------|--------|
| Watch ad | Reroll a failed breed outcome |
| Watch ad | Free mutation attempt |
| Watch ad | Boost income for 10 minutes |
| Watch ad | Reduce heat instantly |

Key: Turning down an ad should never feel like a dumb decision. The base game must be balanced WITHOUT ads.

### 2. Premium Currency (purchased with real money)

Purchased with real money or earned slowly through gameplay.

Spend on:
- Breeding insurance (protect parents from destruction on failure)
- Extra breeding slots (breed more strains simultaneously)
- Cooling boosters and trace scramblers
- Cosmetic strain themes (visual skins for containment lab and strain displays)
- Instant unlocks (skip wait timers on breeding cooldowns)

### 3. Starter Pack ($2.99 one-time)

- Small premium currency amount
- One guaranteed rare starter strain
- One extra breeding slot
- 24-hour income boost

This is the critical first conversion. New players who spend $3 are 10x more likely to spend again. Make it irresistible.

### 4. Battle Pass / "Evolution Pass" (seasonal, $4.99/month or free tier)

Free tier:
- Basic daily rewards
- One mutation attempt per day

Paid tier:
- Daily breeding challenges
- Exclusive cosmetic themes
- Guaranteed rare strain at level 50
- Bonus premium currency throughout the season
- Seasonal strain exclusives (a strain only breedable during this season)

### 5. Cosmetic Packs (pure vanity)

- Containment lab themes (different visual environments for breeding view)
- Strain display customizations (different frames, backgrounds, effects for codex)
- These are pure vanity. Players who love the game buy these to personalize their experience.

## What We Do NOT Do

- No energy systems / forced wait gates
- No paywalls on content
- No mandatory ads
- No pay-to-win mechanics that make free players feel inferior
- No loot boxes with real money (breeding risk is the natural "gamble" mechanic)
