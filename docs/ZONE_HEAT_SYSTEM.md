# LOCALHOST - Zone & Heat System Design

## Zone System

The internet is divided into zones, each with different characteristics. Zones are where you deploy strains to earn data passively.

### Zone Types

| Zone | Security | Payout | Notes |
|------|----------|--------|-------|
| Consumer Networks | Low | Low | Easy to occupy. Good for beginners and volume play. |
| Corporate Servers | Medium | Medium | Need decent stealth to operate safely. |
| Government Networks | High | High | Very risky but very rewarding. |
| Dark Web Nodes | Unpredictable | Variable | Lawless. Strains can thrive or get wiped randomly. |
| Critical Infrastructure | Highest | Highest | Dramatically raises global threat level. |
| Abandoned Servers | Low | Low | Safe. Good for laying low when heat is high. |

### Zone Properties

Each zone shows:
- Defense level (how hard to enter)
- Detection rate (how fast heat rises while occupied)
- Data value (how much you earn)
- Capacity (how many strains it can hold)
- Current occupancy (your strains vs. available space)

### Strategic Decisions

- Deploy a stealth-heavy strain to a high-security zone for steady safe income
- Deploy a speed-heavy strain to a low-security zone for rapid volume income
- Concentrate all power in one zone for maximum output but maximum risk
- Spread thin across many zones for safety but lower total income
- Abandon a zone temporarily to let heat cool down

## Heat / Threat System

Every action generates "heat" -- your global detection level.

### What Raises Heat

- Deploying strains to new zones
- Occupying high-security zones
- Breeding volatile or aggressive strains
- Extracting large amounts of data

### What Lowers Heat

- Staying inactive
- Abandoning zones
- Deploying dormant/stealth strains

### Heat Thresholds

| Level | What Happens |
|-------|-------------|
| Low | Nothing. You're invisible. |
| Medium | Security AI starts scanning your zones. Chance of losing individual strains. |
| High | Targeted countermeasures. Security AI actively attacks. You lose strains and potentially entire zones. |
| Critical | Full raid. You lose everything in one zone and global income takes a massive hit. |

### Strategic Tension

You can't just go full speed all the time. You have to pulse -- push hard, then go quiet. Expand, then consolidate. This creates a rhythm that keeps the game engaging instead of passive.

### Heat Management (Monetization Entry Points)

- Cooling boosters (premium or earned) -- reduce heat faster
- Trace scramblers (premium) -- temporarily hide your activity
- Decoy deployments (earned) -- distract security AI from your real operations
