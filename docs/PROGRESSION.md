# LOCALHOST - Progression Curve

## Early Game (first session, 10-20 minutes)

- Start with one seed strain in one low-security zone
- Earn your first data, breed your first new strain
- Discover your first mutation (guaranteed early discovery for dopamine hit)
- Unlock your second zone
- Feel the excitement of the discovery moment

Goal: Player understands the core loop and experiences the dopamine hit of breeding something new within their first session.

## Mid Game (first few days)

- Several strains in your codex
- 3-5 zones occupied
- First encounter with heat management -- pushed too hard, got raided, learned the lesson
- First legendary strain bred (another dopamine hit)
- Unlock breeding chamber upgrades, more breeding slots
- Battle pass / starter pack offers appear

Goal: Player is hooked on the breeding loop and starts to understand strategic depth. First monetization touchpoint.

## Late Game (weeks/months)

- Deep codex with many strains discovered
- Managing a complex empire across all zone types
- Breeding for specific legendary and mythic strains
- Competing on leaderboards
- Seasonal events with limited-time strains and zones
- Ongoing content updates with new zones, new trait types, new strain categories

Goal: Long-term retention through collection completion, competition, and new content.

## Key Progression Principles

1. Always give the player something to work toward (next strain, next zone, next codex entry)
2. Dopamine hits at regular intervals (new strain discoveries, zone unlocks, first legendary)
3. Difficulty curve through heat management, not through hard walls
4. The game should feel different at hour 1, day 1, week 1, and month 1
5. Late game depth comes from strategic optimization, not just bigger numbers
